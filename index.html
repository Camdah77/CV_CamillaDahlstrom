About Me

What do I do?

My name is Camilla Dahlström. I have run my company Konstlobbyn Sverige AB, which is a colorful meeting place for artists who want to sell and expose their art via a well-visited platform. Konstlobbyn also had a shop in the heart of Täby Centrum for two years. The construction of the website, frontend and backend, E-commerce and automation of membership became an important part of my everyday life, which led me onto a new path. For family reasons, I needed to reduce the intensity of the work and closed store January 2023. In August 2023, I started the education: "Fullstack Software Development" at Code Institute with very good results. In January 2024 I will then be ready for new challenges.

How do I do it?

I follow form as well as function. I know that the aesthetics of effective design combined with application speed, security and reliability combine to create a great user experience.

Why hire me?

I am determined, driven and very creative. With my work experience I can contribute as an important piece of the puzzle in your company. My strength is that I learn quickly and work efficiently to achieve results.
